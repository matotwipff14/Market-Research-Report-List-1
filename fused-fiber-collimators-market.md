<p><p>Fused fiber collimators are optical devices used to collimate light from optical fibers. The market can be divided into two main types: single-mode fiber collimators and multimode fiber collimators. Single-mode fiber collimators are designed for applications requiring high precision and minimal dispersion, making them ideal for long-distance communication systems. In contrast, multimode fiber collimators are suited for applications where light can travel through multiple paths, accommodating short-distance connections in data centers and local area networks, offering greater flexibility in design.</p></p>
