<p><p>Automated sample digestion systems are vital for preparing samples in analytical laboratories. The Microwave Digestion System uses high-pressure vessels and microwave energy to accelerate the digestion process, ensuring efficient breakdown of complex samples. In contrast, the Hotblock Digestion System relies on controlled heating to achieve sample digestion at lower temperatures. Both systems enhance precision, reduce contamination risks, and improve throughput by automating the digestion process, making them essential for applications in environmental, pharmaceutical, and food testing laboratories.</p></p>
