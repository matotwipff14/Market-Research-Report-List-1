<p><p>The Sialic Acid (SA) Assay Kit market is divided into two primary types: colorimetric and fluorometric assays. Colorimetric assays utilize a color change to quantify sialic acid levels in a sample, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, fluorometric assays employ fluorescence detection for higher sensitivity and specificity, making them suitable for low-concentration samples. Both methods serve crucial roles in biomedical research and diagnostic applications, catering to diverse user needs based on sensitivity and detection requirements.</p></p>
