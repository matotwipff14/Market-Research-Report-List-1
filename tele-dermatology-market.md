<p><strong>The Tele Dermatology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Store and Forward Method</li><li>Video Conferencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tele dermatology encompasses two primary market types: the store-and-forward method and video conferencing. The store-and-forward method involves capturing and transmitting dermatological images and patient data to specialists for evaluation at a later time, allowing for asynchronous consultations. In contrast, video conferencing facilitates real-time interaction between patients and dermatologists, enabling immediate discussions and diagnoses. Both methods enhance access to dermatological care, improve patient outcomes, and streamline the referral process, catering to various patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
