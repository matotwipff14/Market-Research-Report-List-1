<p><p>Ventilator circuits can be classified into three market types: open, semi-open, and closed. </p><p>In an open market, the system allows for gas exchange with the environment, facilitating ventilation without restrictions. A semi-open market offers limited exchange, allowing some environmental interaction while maintaining certain controls. The closed market provides a sealed system, ensuring complete containment of gases and minimizing exposure to external elements. Each type serves specific clinical needs and influences patient safety, infection control, and treatment efficiency.</p></p>
