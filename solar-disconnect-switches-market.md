<p><p>The solar disconnect switches market encompasses two primary types: AC and DC disconnect switches. AC disconnect switches are designed to isolate alternating current in solar energy systems, ensuring safe maintenance and protection from overloads. In contrast, DC disconnect switches handle direct current, crucial for managing energy flow from solar panels to inverters. Both types are essential for maintaining system safety, enabling quick disconnection during repairs or emergencies, and ensuring compliance with electrical codes and standards in solar installations.</p></p>
