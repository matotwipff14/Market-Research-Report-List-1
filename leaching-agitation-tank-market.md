<p><p>Leaching agitation tanks are crucial in the extraction process of valuable minerals. The market is categorized into two main types: air agitation tanks and mechanical agitation tanks. Air agitation tanks utilize air bubbles to enhance mixing and promote chemical reactions, making them suitable for lighter materials. In contrast, mechanical agitation tanks rely on mechanical stirrers to ensure thorough mixing of solids and liquids, which is effective for denser materials. Both types aim to optimize leaching processes for improved recovery efficiency.</p></p>
