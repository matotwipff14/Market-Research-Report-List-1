<p><p>The baby incubators market comprises two main types: transport baby incubators and normal baby incubators. Transport baby incubators are designed for mobile use, allowing healthcare professionals to safely transport premature or ill infants during emergencies. These units usually feature compact designs, durable construction, and optimal temperature control. Normal baby incubators, on the other hand, are stationary devices used in neonatal intensive care units to provide a controlled environment for infants, ensuring proper warmth, humidity, and monitoring for health stabilization and recovery.</p></p>
