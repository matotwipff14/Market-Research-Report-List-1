<p><p>The Electronic Health Records (EHR) software market is primarily divided into two types based on deployment: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. **Cloud-based EHRs** allow healthcare providers to access patient records remotely, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT maintenance costs. In contrast, **on-premises EHRs** are installed locally within healthcare facilities, providing greater control over data security and customization, but often requiring higher upfront investments in hardware and ongoing IT support. Both options aim to enhance patient care and streamline healthcare processes.</p></p>
