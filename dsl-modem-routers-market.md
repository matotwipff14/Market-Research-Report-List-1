<p><strong>The DSL Modem Routers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11ac</li><li>802.11b/g/n</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DSL modem routers are categorized based on their wireless standards. The 802.11ac market emphasizes high-speed performance and bandwidth, ideal for activities like streaming and gaming. It offers enhanced range and multi-device connectivity through features like beamforming. In contrast, the 802.11b/g/n market caters to basic internet needs, providing adequate speeds for browsing and light usage. While 802.11b/g/n devices are cost-effective and widely compatible, they lack the advanced capabilities and speed of their 802.11ac counterparts, making them suitable for less demanding environments.</p></p>
