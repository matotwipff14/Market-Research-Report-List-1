<p><p>The Audio Signal Processing ICs market is divided into two main types: Analog Signal Processing ICs and Digital Signal Processing ICs. Analog Signal Processing ICs handle continuous audio signals and are essential for tasks like amplification and filtering, often used in traditional audio systems. In contrast, Digital Signal Processing ICs manipulate digital audio signals using algorithms for functions such as noise reduction, equalization, and audio effects, playing a crucial role in modern digital devices like smartphones and computers, enhancing sound quality and user experience.</p></p>
