CompactFlash II cards, introduced later, include a thicker design that allows for larger storage capacities, extending up to 128 GB or more. Both types are used in various digital devices, including cameras, but CompactFlash II's increased capacity and speed make it more suitable for higher-end applications and professional photography.</p></p>
