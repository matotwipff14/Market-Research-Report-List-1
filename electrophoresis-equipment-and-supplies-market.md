<p><p>The electrophoresis equipment and supplies market is categorized into three main types: Gel Electrophoresis (GE), Capillary Electrophoresis (CE), and Electrophoresis Accessories. GE involves the separation of macromolecules through a gel matrix, widely used in DNA and protein analysis. CE utilizes narrow capillaries to enhance resolution and speed, making it suitable for small biomolecules. Electrophoresis Accessories encompass a variety of tools, such as power supplies, staining agents, and gel tanks, essential for efficient operation and improved accuracy in electrophoresis techniques.</p></p>
