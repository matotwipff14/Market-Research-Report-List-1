<p><p>The strip steel market comprises various types, including hot rolled plain strip, cold rolled plain strip, hot rolled high-quality strip, and cold rolled high-quality strip. Hot rolled plain strips are produced at high temperatures, offering good ductility for structural applications. Cold rolled plain strips, processed at lower temperatures, exhibit smooth surfaces and precise dimensions, ideal for automotive and electrical applications. Hot rolled high-quality strips feature enhanced mechanical properties for demanding uses, while cold rolled high-quality strips provide better surface finishes and tighter tolerances for specialized industries.</p></p>
