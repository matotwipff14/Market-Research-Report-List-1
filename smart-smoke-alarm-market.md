<p><strong>The Smart Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart smoke alarm market includes three primary types: photoelectric, dual sensor, and ionization. Photoelectric alarms utilize a light source and sensor to detect smoke from smoldering fires, minimizing false alarms from cooking. Dual sensor alarms combine photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering comprehensive coverage for both smoldering and flaming fires. Ionization alarms use radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires but can be prone to false alarms. Each type serves distinct needs in ensuring effective fire safety and awareness.</p></p>
