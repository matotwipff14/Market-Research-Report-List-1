<p><p>The pigments for plastics market comprises two primary types: inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are derived from mineral sources and are known for their durability, opacity, and heat resistance. They often provide excellent lightfastness and are commonly used in construction and automotive applications. Conversely, organic pigments are synthesized from carbon-based compounds and offer vibrant colors with more versatility in shades. They are lighter and more transparent but may have lower heat stability, making them suitable for a variety of consumer goods.</p></p>
