<p><p>The Medical Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market encompasses two primary types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic Navigation. </p><p>Hybrid Navigation combines different technologies, such as optical tracking and electromagnetic systems, to enhance surgical accuracy and flexibility. It leverages the strengths of various modalities for improved outcomes. In contrast, Electromagnetic Navigation utilizes electromagnetic fields and sensors to track instruments in real time. This method provides precise guidance during minimally invasive procedures, facilitating better visualization and instrument placement in complex anatomical structures.</p></p>
