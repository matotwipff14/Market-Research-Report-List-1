<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) lighting market encompasses several key components. Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) includes devices like PoE switches and injectors that supply power to connected devices. Controllers and integrated circuits (ICs) manage power distribution, ensuring efficient operation and compliance with standards. Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs are embedded in PoE-enabled lighting fixtures, allowing them to receive and manage power effectively. Together, these elements facilitate the seamless integration of lighting solutions within network infrastructures.</p></p>
