<p><ul><li>Smooth Side</li><li>Texture Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Balance trainers are categorized into two main types: smooth side and textured surface. Smooth side balance trainers provide a stable, uniform surface that allows for controlled movements and stability training, making them ideal for beginners or rehabilitation exercises. Conversely, textured surface balance trainers feature raised patterns or grips that enhance instability, promoting advanced balance training and engaging core muscles more effectively. Both types cater to different fitness levels and training goals, offering versatile options for improving balance and coordination.</p></p>
