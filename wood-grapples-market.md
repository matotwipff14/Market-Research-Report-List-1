<p><p>The wood grapples market primarily features two types: hydraulic and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize hydraulic cylinders to provide powerful grip and movement, offering precision and control, making them ideal for heavy lifting tasks. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on mechanical links and levers to operate, typically being simpler and more durable, suitable for lighter applications. Both types cater to the forestry and construction industries, enabling efficient handling and transport of logs and timber materials.</p></p>
