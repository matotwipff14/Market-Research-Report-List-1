<p><strong>The Jaundice Therapy Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Phototherapy</li><li>Fiberoptic Phototherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jaundice therapy instrument market primarily includes two types: conventional phototherapy and fiberoptic phototherapy. Conventional phototherapy utilizes broad-spectrum light to reduce bilirubin levels in neonates, employing overhead light units. In contrast, fiberoptic phototherapy employs fiberoptic blankets or pads to deliver targeted light therapy directly to the infant’s skin, enhancing comfort and mobility. Both methods aim to effectively manage neonatal jaundice, with fiberoptic therapy offering advantages in terms of space-saving and reduced heat exposure.</p></p>
