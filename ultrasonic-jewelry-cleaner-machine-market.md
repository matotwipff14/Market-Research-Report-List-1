<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Jewelry Cleaner Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic jewelry cleaner machine market is primarily divided into two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive cleaners utilize a magnetostrictive transducer to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, creating ultrasonic waves that effectively remove dirt and grime from jewelry. In contrast, piezoelectric cleaners rely on piezoelectric crystals that oscillate when an electric current is applied, generating similar ultrasonic waves. Both types are effective in cleaning delicate items, but they utilize different mechanisms to achieve similar results in jewelry restoration.</p></p>
