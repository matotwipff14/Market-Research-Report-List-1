<p><p>The agricultural salt market comprises three primary types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater and is rich in minerals, making it valuable for livestock feed and soil enhancement. Well and rock salt, mined from underground deposits, is often used for de-icing and animal nutrition. Lake salt, derived from evaporated saline lakes, is another mineral-rich option, utilized primarily in animal husbandry and agricultural applications. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs.</p></p>
