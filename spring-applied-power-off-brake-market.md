<p><p>The Spring Applied Power Off Brake market includes two primary types: Dualspring and Monospring brakes. Dualspring brakes utilize two springs for a balanced and reliable braking force, often providing enhanced safety and higher torque capacity. In contrast, Monospring brakes consist of a single spring, offering a more compact design suitable for applications with space constraints. Both types are commonly used in industrial machinery and transportation systems, ensuring effective stopping power in power-off scenarios where safety is paramount.</p></p>
