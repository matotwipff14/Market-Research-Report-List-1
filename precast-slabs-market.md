<p><p>The precast slabs market includes prefabricated hollow panels and prefabricated solid panels. Prefabricated hollow panels feature a lightweight design, incorporating voids that reduce material usage while maintaining strength, making them ideal for large-scale projects. Conversely, prefabricated solid panels are robust and provide high durability, suitable for structures requiring enhanced load-bearing capacity. Both types are manufactured off-site, offering quicker installation, reduced labor costs, and streamlined construction processes, contributing to their growing popularity in modern construction practices.</p></p>
