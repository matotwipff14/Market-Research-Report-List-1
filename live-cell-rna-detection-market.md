<p><ul><li>Linear Oligonucleotide Probe</li><li>Linear FRET Probe</li><li>Autoligation FRET Probe</li><li>Molecular Beacon</li><li>MS2-GFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Live Cell RNA Detection market encompasses various probe types used for real-time monitoring of RNA within living cells. Linear Oligonucleotide Probes facilitate direct hybridization with target RNA. Linear FRET Probes use fluorescence resonance energy transfer for detection, while Autoligation FRET Probes enhance signal specificity through enzymatic ligation. Molecular Beacons are designed to fluoresce upon target RNA binding, enabling visual tracking. MS2-GFP combines RNA binding with green fluorescent protein, offering high sensitivity in live cell imaging applications.</p></p>
