<p><ul><li>Bluetooth Transmission</li><li>Network Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless concrete sensor market is primarily categorized into two main types based on transmission methods: Bluetooth transmission and network transmission. Bluetooth transmission enables short-range communication, allowing sensors to connect wirelessly with smartphones or tablets for real-time monitoring of concrete conditions. In contrast, network transmission encompasses broader communication systems, such as Wi-Fi or cellular networks, facilitating long-range data transmission and centralized monitoring. Both types enhance real-time tracking of concrete curing, temperature, and moisture levels, improving construction efficiency and safety.</p></p>
