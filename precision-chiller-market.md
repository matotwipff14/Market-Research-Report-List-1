<p><strong>The Precision Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Chiller</li><li>Centrifugal Chiller</li><li>Reciprocating Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision chiller market encompasses various types including screw chillers, centrifugal chillers, and reciprocating chillers. Screw chillers use a twin-screw compression mechanism, offering reliable cooling efficiency for larger facilities. Centrifugal chillers utilize a rotating impeller to compress refrigerant, ideal for high-capacity applications due to their energy efficiency. Reciprocating chillers operate using pistons, providing precise temperature control for smaller systems. All types cater to specific cooling needs, ensuring effective temperature management in data centers, hospitals, and industrial processes.</p></p>
