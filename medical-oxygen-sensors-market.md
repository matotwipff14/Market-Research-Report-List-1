<p><strong>The Medical Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zirconia Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Optical Oxygen Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical oxygen sensors market includes two main types: zirconia oxygen sensors and optical oxygen sensors. Zirconia oxygen sensors utilize zirconium dioxide to measure oxygen concentration based on ionic conductivity at high temperatures, providing accurate readings in various environments. In contrast, optical oxygen sensors employ fluorescence quenching principles, where the oxygen level is determined by the intensity of emitted light from fluorescent materials. Both sensor types play crucial roles in medical applications, ensuring appropriate oxygen levels for patient safety and effective care.</p></p>
