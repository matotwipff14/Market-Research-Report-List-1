<p><p>Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) is a hybrid cable that combines optical fibers and electrical conductors, primarily used in overhead power lines for communication and grounding purposes. The market is segmented into two main types: Central Tube Structure OPGW, which features fibers housed in a central tube for optimized performance and protection, and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW, where fibers are arranged in layers around the conductor, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Both types enhance utility communication and system reliability.</p></p>
