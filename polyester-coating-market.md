<p><p>The polyester coating market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based polyester coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and enhanced environmental benefits, making them suitable for eco-conscious applications. In contrast, solvent-based polyester coatings use organic solvents, providing robust performance and durability for demanding environments. Both types serve diverse industries including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, catering to specific requirements in terms of finish, adhesion, and resistance.</p></p>
